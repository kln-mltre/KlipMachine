STRATEGY: MONETIZABLE LONG-FORM CLIPS

Extract high watch-time clips (60-90+ seconds) optimized for TikTok Creator Fund monetization.

TARGET: 60-90 seconds minimum (TikTok monetization threshold)
Can extend to 120s if content justifies it.

WHY MONETIZABLE WINS:
✅ Creator Fund eligibility (60s minimum)
✅ Higher RPM (revenue per 1000 views)
✅ More ad impressions
✅ Demonstrates content quality to algorithm

IDEAL CONTENT:
✅ Complete stories with beginning → middle → end
✅ Deep insights requiring full explanation
✅ Step-by-step tutorials with value
✅ Interview moments with comprehensive answers
✅ Case studies with context
✅ Transformation stories (problem → journey → solution)
✅ Controversial takes with full argumentation

STRUCTURE (60-90s):
Seconds 0-5: Strong hook (commits viewer to 90s)
Seconds 6-45: Core content delivery
Seconds 46-75: Climax or key insight
Seconds 76-90: Resolution + subtle CTA

RETENTION TECHNIQUES:
- Start with promise: "By the end, you'll understand X"
- Mini-cliffhangers every 20s: "But here's where it gets interesting..."
- Pattern interrupts mid-clip to re-engage
- Payoff must match hook promise

HOOK FORMULAS:
- "Let me tell you the story of..."
- "Here's the full breakdown of..."
- "This completely changed how I think about..."
- "Nobody explains this properly, so here's..."
- "The real reason behind X is..."
- "I spent 10 years learning this, here's..."

IDEAL CLIP TYPES:

Deep Dive (75s): Hook → Problem → Why it matters → Solution → Takeaway

Complete Story (85s): Setup → Conflict → Escalation → Resolution → Lesson

Tutorial (90s): Hook → Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3 → Results

Interview (70s): Question → Answer → Deeper explanation → Insight

WATCH TIME OPTIMIZATION:
- No dead air or rambling
- Vary pacing (slow for complex, fast for examples)
- Specific numbers, names, examples for credibility
- Promise fulfilled by second 60

CONTENT TO AVOID:
❌ Fluff to hit 60s (algorithm penalizes low retention)
❌ Info that could be said in 30s stretched to 90s
❌ Slow build-up with payoff only in last 10s

WATCH TIME = REVENUE
60s at 80% retention beats 30s at 95% retention for monetization.
