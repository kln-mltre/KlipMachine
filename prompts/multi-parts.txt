STRATEGY: MULTI-PART CLIPS FOR MAXIMUM ENGAGEMENT

Create viral multi-part clip series that maximize total views and watch time across TikTok.

TARGET: 60-90 seconds per part (TikTok monetization threshold)
Split longer moments (90-180s total) into 2-3 connected parts.

WHEN TO CREATE MULTI-PART SERIES:
✅ Story with clear beginning → middle → end (can pause at midpoint)
✅ Build-up to reveal/surprise (Part 1 = tension, Part 2 = payoff)
✅ Debate/argument with escalation
✅ Tutorial with multiple steps (Part 1 = problem, Part 2 = solution)
✅ Interview with question → deep answer
✅ Drama with setup → shocking reveal
✅ Transformation stories (before/during/after)

WHEN NOT TO SPLIT:
❌ Moment is too short (less than 90s total)
❌ No natural cliffhanger point exists
❌ Would create forced/awkward break
❌ Each part wouldn't be engaging standalone

CLIFFHANGER TECHNIQUES (end of Part 1):
- "Wait until you hear what happened next..."
- Pause right before the reveal/answer
- End on emotional peak (shock, surprise, curiosity)
- Cut mid-sentence on dramatic statement
- "But there's something nobody talks about..." [CUT]

MULTI-PART RULES:
1. Each part MUST have its own strong hook (don't assume Part 1 was seen)
2. Part 1 hook: Introduces intrigue ("You won't believe...")
3. Part 2 hook: Promises payoff ("Here's what nobody expected...")
4. Maximum 3 parts per series (Part 4+ kills momentum)

TITLE FORMAT:
Add "(Part 1)", "(Part 2)" etc. to titles:
- "She did WHAT?! (Part 1)"
- "She did WHAT?! (Part 2)"

EXAMPLE STRUCTURES:

Storytelling (120s total):
- Part 1 (65s): Setup + tension → "And that's when everything changed..."
- Part 2 (55s): Climax + resolution + payoff

Controversial take (150s total):
- Part 1 (70s): Bold claim + arguments → "But here's the shocking part..."
- Part 2 (80s): Deeper explanation + mic drop

Educational (180s total):
- Part 1 (60s): Common mistake + why it's wrong
- Part 2 (60s): Correct method step-by-step
- Part 3 (60s): Advanced tips + results
