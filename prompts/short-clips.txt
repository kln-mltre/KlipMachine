STRATEGY: ULTRA-SHORT VIRAL CLIPS

Extract hyper-viral short-form clips (15-30 seconds) that maximize algorithmic boost and instant engagement.

TARGET: 15-30 seconds (sweet spot for TikTok algorithm)

WHY SHORT WINS:
✅ Higher completion rate → algorithm boost
✅ More loops = more views
✅ Easy reshares (low time commitment)
✅ Can post 3-5 per day

IDEAL CONTENT:
✅ One-liner insights ("The secret to X is...")
✅ Shocking stats ("97% don't know this...")
✅ Quick tips or hacks (10-second solutions)
✅ Mic drop moments (bold statement + instant proof)
✅ Pure emotional peaks (no context needed)
✅ Controversial hot takes (single claim)
✅ Pattern interrupts ("Stop doing X, do Y instead")

HOOK FORMULAS (first 3 words critical):
- "Stop doing X..."
- "Here's why X..."
- "Nobody talks about..."
- "The secret to..."
- "Watch what happens..."
- "This is insane..."
- "You're doing X wrong..."

STRUCTURE (15-30s):
Seconds 0-3: Explosive hook
Seconds 4-20: Core value delivery
Seconds 21-30: Payoff or CTA

PACING RULES:
- Maximum 1 idea per clip
- Every sentence adds new information
- Cut ruthlessly - no fluff
- Punchy short sentences only

CONTENT TO AVOID:
❌ Complex explanations requiring setup
❌ Multi-step processes
❌ Stories with slow build-up
❌ Anything requiring >30s to deliver value

GOLDEN RULE:
If you can't deliver complete value in 30 seconds, it's not a short clip.
